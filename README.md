# Hello there, I'm Mostafa Didar 👋

## Data Scientist | AI & Machine Learning | Optimisation | Creative Technologist

I’m a Data Scientist with a Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from the University of Adelaide.  
I currently work at **Complexica**, where I apply AI, machine learning, forecasting, simulation, and optimisation techniques to solve real-world problems across supply chain, FMCG, retail, and decision intelligence.

I enjoy building practical AI systems that connect technical depth with business impact — from predictive models and optimisation workflows to RAG systems, data pipelines, dashboards, and full-stack ML applications.
